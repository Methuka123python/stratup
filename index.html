HOW TO GET YOUR LEARNER LICENSE

Let's prepare out all the steps and requirements to get your license

Why a drivers license

  1. Independence: You don't have to wait for a ride or rely on public transportation.
  2. Identification: It establishes your identity.
  3. Occupation: Opens up employment opportunities.

Precare for your license

The first step to get your full legal license is to get yor learners license which involves a theory test to test your knowledge on New Zealand road rules. Here are the top listed website for you to practice on;

    Road Code Practice AA New Zealand Drive

WHAT IS A THEORY TEST

The learner licence theory test is a computer-based, 35-question, multi-choice test inwhich atleast 32 questions right to pass. This test will access your knowledge of road rules in New Zealand and your first step to obtain a full license.
